Covid do's and dont's

DO'S
  • Wear a mask and gloves while going out.
  • Wash your hands frequently, if not atleast use a sanitizer.
  • Maintain 1 metre distance.
  • Get vaccinated.
  • Stay home if you are sick.
  • Visit a doctor when you have symtoms.
  • Get a test when you are in contact with a person who is positive for covid.
Dont's
  • Touch nose, mouth and eyes.
  • Go out without a reason.
  • Remove a mask when you are out.
  • Spit on the roads.
  • Make a crowd.
